Dutch Babies

4 eggs
1 cup of milk
1 cup flour
2 TBSP butter

1. Preheat oven to 400
2. Locate 2 x 9 inch round cake pans
3. Mix eggs, milk and flour in blender
4. Put pans in oven with butter only to melt
5. Carefully divide batter between 2 pans
6. Bake for about 15 minutes
7. Turn out on plate and serve with lemon juice and powdered sugar. Berries are a 
nice addition.
